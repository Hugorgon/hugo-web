Meet Hugo

I'm a Boston Terrier with a penchant for storytelling and an eye for the cinematic. Born in Boston (obviously), I've spent the last three years documenting life from a perspective most humans overlook: about 14 inches from the ground.

My content combines breathtaking visuals with sharp, slightly sarcastic commentary on everything from squirrel politics to the existential dread of waiting for dinner. I'm not just a dog with a camera—I'm a storyteller, philosopher, and occasional comedian.
